## The Five Movements

| Movement | Subtitle | Core Concept |
|----------|----------|-------------|
| 1 | The Question | Three irreconcilable voices: Viola C / Cello C♯ / Soprano B♮ |
| 2 | The Weight of Waiting | Funeral march + hidden "Freude" fragment |
| 3 | The Voice Fails | 21 bars → the voice descends and vanishes |
| 4 | The Ghost of Heroism | Eroica opening × *dona nobis pacem* |
| **5** | **[The audience performs]** | **Not notated. The conductor does not move.** |

> *"Lassen Sie das Publikum entscheiden."*  
> (Let the audience decide.)
